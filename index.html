0xCastle
Research Retreat

After ETHBerlin 2024
27.05. - 31.05.
Abstract

The 0xCastle Research Retreat is a 5 day event for researchers, developers and enthusiasts to work on their projects in a collaborative environment. The event is hosted in a castle in the countryside of Germany. The castle is equipped with a large conference room, a library, a kitchen and a large garden. The event is free of charge and open to everyone. The event is organized by the ETHBerlin team.
