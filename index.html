0 xCastle

Research Retreat

Post-ETHBerlin
May 26th - 31st 2024

Abstract

0xCastle is a 5-day program focused on blockchain education and research, set in a historic castle near Leipzig, Germany. A selected group of researchers, developers, and technical blockchain enthusiasts will convene post-ETHBerlin (May 26-31) to educate, research, and connect.

Focused on pivotal blockchain issues in topics like privacy, scalability, and interoperability, the retreat integrates a mix of talks, workshops, and research groups. Tailored for knowledge sharing between participants, the schedule enables interdisciplinary brainstorming on current research questions.

1. Background

The 0xCastle team had a transformative experience at 0xVillage in Turkey that inspired us to pursue 0xCastle. We were deeply moved by the commitment to the blockchain ethos, not only in tech but also in the community's mindset. Every interaction echoed a shared vision of steering blockchain towards greater trustlessness, transparency, and privacy. It made us realize how those values are getting lost in the culture of web3, the applications built, and how we communicate with each other.

Decentralization must transcend beyond a marketing buzzword for websites and tweets. 0xCastle emerges as a response - a call to the technical community to embody and reinforce the principles of blockchain. Through a blend of peer-to-peer teaching, open dialogue, and collective problem-solving, we will navigate the forefront of blockchain innovation; steadily aligning our ideations with the core principles.

Our team, with its experience in organizing events like 0xVillage, TUM Blockchain Conference, Ledgerfest, and many more, is committed to fostering an authentic and impactful experience. Given these seeds, 0xCastle serves as more than a platform for education and research; it's a journey towards first-principle thinking in our work, connections, and inner selves.

2. Event Overview

The heart of 0xCastle lies in the spontaneous and unrestrained interactions. It is designed to encourage free-flowing conversation and idea exchange, whether through formal sessions or leisurely activities in the castle's inspiring environment. This balanced mix of focused education and relaxed brainstorming paves the way for organic inspirations and cross-disciplinary collaborations, making 0xCastle a true opportunity for blockchain advancement.

2.1 Content

The 0xCastle Research Retreat, unfolding over five days, welcomes a select group of tech-savvy blockchain enthusiasts from academia and industry. Participants will be immersed in a comprehensive program focused on learning, teaching, research, and connection. The carefully selected talks, workshops, and research sessions will be delivered from a vetted lineup of attendees, educational collaborators, speakers, and sponsors. Their difficulty spans from introductory talks to in-depth research discussions, covering topics like cryptography (ZK, MPC, FHE), layer 2 & rollups, disaggregated blockchain layers, mechanism design, and more, depending on expressed interest. This unique participant-driven approach ensures an interdisciplinary exchange of knowledge and ideas, where each attendee not only absorbs knowledge but also contributes to the collective wisdom.

2.2 Logistics

After ETH Berlin, we'll shuttle attendees from Berlin to the castle near Leipzig, or you can travel directly if not attending the hackathon. The retreat features all-inclusive meals and beverage selections, along with flexible accommodation ranging from dorms to private rooms ($60 to $250 per night), emphasizing inclusivity with financial aid for students.

3. Attendees

In this section, we will detail the different groups of present attendees, why this event is valuable to them, and how they can participate.

3.1 Participants

0xCastle welcomes 50 participants, with a 40% academic and 60% industry composition. Attendees will have expertise in technical fields like computer science and mathematics, focused on advancing blockchain technology. Applicants are encouraged to propose topics they are passionate about presenting at the retreat. We seek applicants who are committed to innovation and eager to make substantial contributions to the advancement of blockchain technology.

3.2. Partners

TUM Blockchain Club

Blockchain Acceleration Foundation

0xCastle allocates some slots for educational and community partners. Beyond participation, educational non-profits, such as university blockchain clubs, can apply to present their organization's research at the retreat.

3.3 Speakers

Speaker selection at 0xCastle is invite-only, targeting individuals who can significantly enhance our program with their in-depth knowledge and research.

3.4 Sponsors

We warmly encourage sponsors who wish to support our commitment to research and education to contact us. Your involvement enhances educational opportunities and accessibility, particularly enabling student participation without financial barriers.

5. The Castle

❮ ❯

The CryptoCastle of Burgscheidungen, a beacon of Baroque architecture, traces its roots to the 6th century as a speculated royal seat of the Thuringian Empire. Today, it serves as a vibrant meeting point for technology and blockchain enthusiasts, offering a rich tapestry of facilities, including a spa, workshop, and gala rooms, diverse accommodations, a rave basement, and a bar, all encircled by expansive parklands and a river. Its diverse activities, from volleyball to historical tours through castle gardens, make it an exemplary venue for blending intellectual engagement with leisure.
