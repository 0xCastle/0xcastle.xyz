Abstract

The 0xCastle Research Retreat is a collaborative platform emphasizing education and research on blockchain technology. Subsequent to ETH Berlin, approximately 50 researchers, developers, and blockchain enthusiasts will live in a historic castle near Leipzig, Germany, for a five-day program. There, we will offer an environment for in-depth learning and discussions on current technological advancements in privacy, scalability, and more. The retreat's framework, coupled with the diverse and historic castle environment, is designed to stimulate engaging brainstorming sessions among selected participants. Our sponsors allow us to reduce the room cost, serve food, and provide financial aid for students.

This confluence of minds is not just about bringing together experts, but about nurturing the rising stars who will shape the future of technology. To facilitate this, we have chosen extraordinary settings for our retreats, places that inspire unconventional thinking and creativity. Our mission is clear: to create an environment that is conducive to the free flow of ideas, where intellectual curiosity is nurtured, and innovative solutions to complex problems are born. 2. 0xCastle Research Retreat

2.1 Content

The 0xCastle Research Retreat is a five-day program for selected tech-savvy applicants from academia and industry. Participants will learn, educate, research, and hack in a schedule that is curated to cover and advance pressing topics aligned with the interest and knowledge base of participants. With varying depth and intensity of different topics, there will be anything form introductory-based talks to educational series and research groups. Content will be presented by participants themselves based on their application and evaluated topic of expertise, invited speakers, and sponsors through presentations, workshops, and other activities.

While a framework can provide many benefits for technological (Auseinandersetzungen), most ideas emerge from (ungewungene) discussions and exchange. Therefore, besides an educational program, the castle offers a space for connecting and brainstorming with each other through various group activities. There will be enough free time to engage with each other in wellness activities, games, cocktail hours, and more surprises to be discovered.

2.2 Logistics

As soon as Eth Berlin ends, a shuttle will bring the selected participants from Berlin to the Castle near Leipzig, where they will live for five days. Next to the prepared schedule, participants will enjoy three meals per day and have drinks and activities included in their ticket. Selected participants can choose between different room options of dorm, double room and single room, either with shared or private bathroom. Depending on the chosen package, the price varies between 70 EUR to 250 EUR per night. To create an accessible event, we offer financial aid for students, supported by our sponsors.

4. Call for Participation

4.1 Attendees

With a limited capacity of about 50 participants, 0xCastle will host about 40% from academia and 60% from industry, although most industry professionals will have a strong academic background. All participants will have a technical background in, e.g., computer science, math, or a related topic, and are currently working on and researching cutting-edge technology in the blockchain space. These topics can be anything from, but not limited to, zero knowledge cryptography, modular blockchains, account abstraction, etc.

We invite anyone with such background to apply to this program to pursue education and research with peers. Potential participants working in academia or students are invited to apply for financial aid as well to waive their participation cost.

Apply Now! Be as elaborate as possible in your application and show us that you have the mindset and knowledge to push this space forward in the next years.

4.2. Speakers

Due to the very limited spots we have available for speakers, coming as a speaker is on an invite-only basis. We will only invite speakers who will be able to add exceptional value to our program by educating participants in their demonstrated area of expertise, as well as pose and guide pressing research questions.

4.3 Partners

We believe that decentralization not only applies to the technology we use, but also to the education we create and research we pursue. Information should not be made available by only one source. Therefore, as an educational initiative, we have several non-profit partners that will support our educational content. Currently (umfassen) the TUM Blockchain Club and Blockchain Acceleration Foundation, any educational institution focussed on blockchain is welcome to contribute.

If you are a blockchain club from a university or another non-profit educational initiative, we welcome your email for participation. Partners will get a few spots to achieve a collaborative environment of as many universities and institutions as possible. Members of those institutions are welcome to apply as participants and mention the interest of their initiative as well.

4.4 Sponsors

While monetary support is much needed to make such an initiative possible, our selection process is thorough as any sponsor participating must add educational value to our program. Furthermore, no advertisement for specific ecosystems beyond brand placement will be allowed. This applies specifically to talks, workshops, and other presentations where the focus should be entirely on technical aspects.

If you want to support the event with financial means to allow smart people to focus on research & education, we welcome your email. You will strongly contribute to educational freedom and allow students to come for free.
